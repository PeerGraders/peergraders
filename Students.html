Join Us As A Student Today!

AP™ exams for indeed tough. The FRQ portion is especially difficult for a lot of students and is tough for teachers to grade. 

Fear not! Peer Graders offers 100% free AP™ FRQ response grading & feedback services to students all year round from certified, trained AP™ volunteer graders who have gotten a 5 in the respective exam! Let us be part of your journey of getting a 5 in May!

Currently Offering Grading Services for: 
AP™ English Language & Composition, AP™ US History, AP™ World History: Modern, AP™ European History, and AP™ Seminar 

Grading Reports from Peer Graders

All volunteer graders at Peer Graders use official AP™ rubric published by the College Board™ for each exams. We provide detailed comments and justifications for every rubric row on the grading reports and provide a overall feedback at the end of the grading report.

All grading reports are filled out by a volunteer grader and then is vetted and verified by a senior volunteer grader to ensure the quality and accuracy of grading reports. 

Students are able to receive grading reports of their submitted AP™ FRQ work within 5 business days! 

How Peer Graders Work

For Prospective AP™ Students!

01

User Registration

All students must go through user registration as Peer Graders is a platform open exclusively to HS students. User approval takes 5 business days. Go the "Students" tab and fill out the form!

02

Subject Selection

Peer Graders currently offer free grading services to five AP™ subjects. Select the AP™ subject of which you would like to receive grading. 

03

Submit FRQ Response

Submit your FRQ response on the designated form. You MUST provide the prompt/stimulus materials along with your FRQ response. Your response can be either handwritten or typed. 

04

Grading Report

Within 5 business days, a grading report will be sent to your email. Grading report consists of personalized overall feedback and overall score based on the AP™ rubric.

User Registration Form

Fill this form out and we'll get back to you! You will have access to all features of this website including the Dashboard once the user registration is completed! This procedure is necessary as Peer Graders is a platform that is open to exclusively High School students preparing for AP™ exams.
