Join Us As A Volunteer Grader Today!

There are 8 million low-income secondary students in the U.S. Most can't afford private grading services provided by large test prep companies for AP™ FRQ responses due to its high price which starts at $20 and easily reaches over $100 for detailed feedback. 

This is why AP™ score disparity is apparent based on socioeconomic background. Without academic support, students face setbacks and challenges that will limit their opportunities to receive higher education. 

Why Volunteer at Peer Graders? 

Flexibility

  • Have complete control over your volunteer schedule!
  • Once approved as a volunteer grader, you are expected to complete two grading reports every week at any time you would like.

Real Impact

  • Have the opportunity to positively influence a student's life trajectory with tangible AP™ score results!
  • Increase students' chances of higher education enrollment by providing detailed AP™ FRQ response feedback!

Benefits of Becoming a Volunteer Grader

Professional Development (Training & Resources)

  • Thorough AP™ Reader Training & Certification Course Offered by Peer Graders to All Volunteer Graders (Manadatory Requirement)
  • Multiple examples of past scoring reports and scoring guidelines are stored as resources
  • Thorough feedback offered by senior volunteer graders every week

Join an Active Community of Volunteers & Students

  • Interact with hundreds of fellow volunteer graders in our private Discord community to ask questions, share stories, and network! 
  • Attend monthly volunteer grader meetings for tips/networking

Earn Verified Volunteer Hours & Reference Letters

  • Earn one hour of volunteer hours every week by completing two grading reports! An additional hour can be earn upon completing two extra grading reports
  • Verified, signed volunteer letter is offered when requested which can be used in college/scholarship applications
  • Reference letters are offered to volunteer graders who regularly attends our monthly meetings when they have 50+ volunteer hours (can be added as a recommendation letter for college apps)

Prerequisites of a Volunteer Grader

Although we would love to have everyone interested onboard, we unfortunately have strict prerequisites to ensure to quality of grading reports we offer to prospective AP™ students. 

  • Received a Score of 5 in any of the following AP™ exams: English Language & Composition, US History, World History: Modern, European History, Seminar (AP Score Report Submission is Required)
  • Willingness to complete AP™ reader training & certification course (minimum of three hours of contact hours)
  • Willingness to complete at least two gradings every week (you will receive one service hour for completing two gradings) 

Is it your first time grading AP™ FRQ responses? 

Fear not, our AP™ reader training and certification course will equip you with all the necessary skills you need to grade AP™ FRQ responses. Also, all grading reports are vetted and validated by our senior volunteer graders at Peer Graders before they are sent out to students, so fear not!
